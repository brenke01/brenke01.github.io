2014 Minnesota Twins Preview

As yet another tough season came to a close in 2013, the Minnesota Twins are looking to their prospects for a solution

The Minnesota Twins have many young starters to hopefully fix the problematic pitching rotation

Some notable new pitchers and prospects that hope to make a name for themselves include:

  1. Phil Hughes: RHP acquired from the New York Yankees
  2. Alex Meyer: RHP ranked in the top 100 prospects of 2014
  3. Trevor May: RHP ranked in the top 100 prosepcts of 2014

Two of the biggest prospects in the last five years hope to make their way on to the roster this year. Outfielder Byron Buxton has been ranked the number 1 prospect in the entire nation. To join him is 3B Miguel Sano, a power hitter that could find himself in the Twins lineup as soon as this June.
